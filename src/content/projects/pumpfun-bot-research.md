## Overview
Researched Pump.fun token launch platform mechanics on Solana, analyzing bot trading strategies, sniping mechanisms, and MEV extraction patterns in the Solana memecoin ecosystem.

## Research Areas

### Platform Mechanics
- Analyzed Pump.fun bonding curve implementation and pricing model
- Studied token graduation process to Raydium liquidity pools
- Evaluated transaction ordering and priority fee dynamics on Solana

### Bot Strategy Analysis
- Researched sniping bot architectures for new token launches
- Analyzed copy-trading and wallet tracking strategies
- Studied bundle transaction patterns and Jito block engine usage
- Evaluated anti-rug detection heuristics

### MEV on Solana
- Compared Solana MEV landscape with Ethereum (Jito vs Flashbots)
- Analyzed sandwich attack patterns on Solana DEXs
- Studied validator-level transaction ordering and tip mechanisms

## Technologies
- **Blockchain**: Solana, Raydium, Jupiter
- **Languages**: Rust, TypeScript
- **Infrastructure**: Jito, Solana RPC, WebSocket feeds

## Impact
Research provided insights into Solana DeFi trading dynamics and bot ecosystem mechanics relevant to trading tool development.
