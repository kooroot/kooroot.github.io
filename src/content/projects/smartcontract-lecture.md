## Overview
Course materials and smart contract examples developed for a smart contract development lecture series at Korea University in partnership with Onther. Structured as a progressive curriculum covering Solidity fundamentals through advanced contract patterns.

## Curriculum
- Solidity fundamentals and EVM basics
- Smart contract design patterns
- Testing and deployment with Hardhat
- Security best practices and common vulnerabilities
