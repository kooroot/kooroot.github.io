## Overview
A collection of smart contract security audit work covering DEX and Lending protocol implementations from DreamplusAcademy, along with an independent audit of the TON staking v2 protocol. Focused on identifying vulnerabilities and ensuring contract safety.

## Key Research

### DEX Protocol Audits
- Audited 13 different DEX contract implementations
- Analyzed AMM logic, liquidity pool security, and fee mechanisms
- Identified common vulnerability patterns in swap functions

### Lending Protocol Audits
- Audited 12 different Lending contract implementations
- Reviewed collateral management and liquidation logic
- Assessed oracle dependency and price manipulation risks

### TON Staking v2 Audit
- Independent security audit of TON staking protocol
- Foundry-based testing and verification
- Staking mechanism and reward distribution analysis

## Technologies
- **Smart Contracts**: Solidity, Foundry
- **Security Tools**: Static analysis, manual review
- **Frameworks**: Hardhat, Foundry (Forge)

## Results
Completed comprehensive audit reports for 25+ smart contract implementations across DEX, Lending, and Staking protocols, contributing to DeFi security best practices.
